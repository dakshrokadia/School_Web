Introduction

Mountain Biking is an adventurous sport that involves a person who rides a bike down trails. There are many different types of trails such as: flow which is smooth, techy which is rocky, jumps which has lots of jumps and many more in between. Mountain Biking - also abbreviated with MTB - is very broad in terms of competitions and has many different types competitions and races.
