    Software Developer - Career information

    General Information

    Becoming a Software Developer is a great option as it has good pay and will most likely not be taken over by Artificial Intelligence in the future. The pay is anywhere between $50k to $200k which is quite a big range. Training usually takes 1-3 years. Some other specialities such as developing website include developing and promoting a technical plan, suggesting necessary equipment and costs, maintain the website and upgrading to better systems.

    What you will do

    The jobs involves discussing ideas and projects with teammates. You have to design programs, write programs, run and test programs and maintain / upgrade existing programs.

    Useful Skills and Knowledge

    It is crucial to have knowledge and experience for computer software and systems, programming languages and techniques, hardware experience, data security and protection.

    Useful Video / Links


    Summary

    To summarise, Becoming a software developer is a great course and is future proof, good paying, and relatively fun. There is a lot of computer and software involved as that is the biggest part of the job. Not only is it programming but also problem solving and finding new ways to achieve something. You usually work as a team and work at a office, though some may have an option to work from home.
